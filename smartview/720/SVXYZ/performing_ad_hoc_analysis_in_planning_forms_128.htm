Performing Ad Hoc Analysis in Planning Forms

If you have been assigned the ad hoc user role by the administrator, you can perform ad hoc analysis on Oracle Hyperion Planning forms that have been enabled for ad hoc by the administrator.

To perform ad hoc analysis in Planning forms:

  1. Open the form.
  2. Do one of the following:

    • From the Smart View ribbon, click Analyze. This button is enabled only if the current form has been enabled for ad hoc analysis.

    • Select the form in the Smart View Panel and click Ad hoc analysis in the Action Panel.

  3. See Ad Hoc Analysis for information about performing ad hoc analysis.
